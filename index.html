Rethink the way you Drink

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), contaminated water accounts for 80% of all diseases contracted during travel.
Travelers, who feel the local drinking water is not potable, contribute greatly to bottle consumption, consuming up to 6+ bottles a day.
2.7 MILLION TONS of water bottle plastic is used across the globe anually.
In many countries, there is no proper disposal method for empty bottles. These bottles, which are made of hazardous petroleum-based plastic, then accumulate in the land and water as non-biodegradable rubbish.
